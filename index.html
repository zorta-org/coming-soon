👨‍🎓 I’m under 18. Can I still use Zorta?

Absolutely. Users aged 13+ can build projects, join communities, collaborate, freelance, and earn rewards on Zorta, subject to our Terms of Service and local laws.

🌍 Why does Zorta use reward-based payouts?

Many talented builders—especially students and younger creators—don’t have access to bank accounts or traditional payment methods. Zorta was designed so your ability to earn isn’t limited by your age or financial setup. If you can build, you should be able to get rewarded.

💜 Built for the Next Generation of Builders

13+ can earn. No bank account required. Redeem your earnings through gift cards, Discord Nitro, games, in-game currency, and more—because talent shouldn’t be limited by traditional payment systems.
